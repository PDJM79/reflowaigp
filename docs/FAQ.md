# ReflowAI GP — Frequently Asked Questions

---

## Practice Manager

**Q: What is ReflowAI GP?**
A: ReflowAI GP is a compliance and governance platform built for UK GP practices. It manages IPC audits, complaints, HR records, fire safety, and regulatory tasks for CQC (England), HIW (Wales), and HIS (Scotland), and uses AI to surface gaps before an inspection finds them.

**Q: How do I set up my practice for the first time?**
A: Sign up, enter your practice details, and select your regulatory body (CQC, HIW, or HIS). ReflowAI GP configures your compliance framework based on the regulator you choose, so your task lists and audit templates match the standards that apply to you.

**Q: We operate across England and Wales — can we manage both from one account?**
A: Yes. You can add multiple practice sites under **Settings → Locations** and assign each one a different regulator. Each site has its own compliance tasks and reports while sharing one management account.

**Q: How does the AI suggestions feature work?**
A: After you complete audits or log activity, the AI analyses your data against regulatory requirements and flags areas of risk — for example, overdue IPC checks or incomplete HR records — with suggested actions to resolve them.

**Q: Can I trust the AI suggestions for regulatory decisions?**
A: AI suggestions are a prompt, not a determination. They highlight potential gaps based on your records, but a qualified team member should always review and confirm before acting on any compliance recommendation.

**Q: How does the platform handle CQC, HIW, and HIS differences?**
A: Each regulator has different frameworks, audit criteria, and evidence standards. ReflowAI GP maintains separate template sets for each and applies the correct one automatically based on the regulator assigned to each practice location.

**Q: How do I manage complaints within the platform?**
A: Go to **Complaints → Log Complaint**, enter the details, and the platform creates a tracked workflow covering acknowledgement, investigation, response, and closure. Every step is timestamped and stored for regulatory review.

**Q: How does HR management work?**
A: Staff profiles store contracts, DBS check dates, training records, and mandatory certification expiry dates. The platform alerts you before anything lapses so you are never caught with an out-of-date record at inspection.

**Q: How do I export an evidence pack for a CQC inspection?**
A: Go to **Reports → Inspection Pack**, select the location and date range, and click **Export PDF**. The pack compiles your audit results, completed tasks, complaints log, and HR evidence into a regulator-ready document.

**Q: How is billing managed?**
A: ReflowAI GP is billed by subscription through Stripe, typically per practice site. Invoices and plan details are available under **Settings → Billing**.

## Administrator

**Q: How do I add staff members and assign their roles?**
A: Go to **Settings → Team**, enter the staff member's email, and select their role — Practice Manager, Administrator, Nurse Lead, or Reception. They will receive an invitation email with login instructions.

**Q: What can each role see and do?**
A: **Practice Manager** has full access to all modules and settings. **Administrator** manages tasks, records, and reports but cannot change billing or user roles. **Nurse Lead** owns IPC audits and clinical compliance tasks. **Reception** logs complaints and manages their assigned administrative tasks only.

**Q: How do I set up fire safety compliance tasks?**
A: Go to **Tasks → Fire Safety**, and either use the pre-built template (weekly alarm tests, monthly extinguisher checks, annual evacuation drill) or create custom tasks. Each task can be assigned to a named individual with a recurring schedule.

**Q: What happens when a compliance deadline is missed?**
A: Overdue items are flagged in red on the Dashboard and the assigned staff member receives an alert. Practice Managers also receive a daily digest of anything outstanding across the practice.

**Q: Can I customise audit templates?**
A: Yes. Go to **Audits → Templates**, select a template, and click **Customise**. You can add, remove, or reword questions while keeping the template linked to the correct regulatory standard.

**Q: How do I record and track claims?**
A: Go to **Claims**, select the claim type (e.g., QOF, Enhanced Service, local incentive scheme), and complete the required fields. The platform tracks submission status and flags any claims approaching their deadline.

**Q: How do I generate a monthly governance report?**
A: Go to **Reports → Governance Summary**, choose the month and location, and click **Generate**. The report covers completed audits, open complaints, overdue tasks, and HR compliance in one document.

## Nurse Lead

**Q: What is an IPC audit and why do I need to do one?**
A: An IPC (Infection Prevention and Control) audit is a structured check of your practice's hygiene and infection control standards, required by CQC, HIW, and HIS. ReflowAI GP provides templates aligned to each regulator so you always audit against the right criteria.

**Q: How do I start an IPC audit?**
A: Go to **Audits → IPC**, select your practice location, and click **Start Audit**. Work through the questions on screen, adding notes and photos where required, then submit. The result is saved automatically and included in future inspection packs.

**Q: How often do I need to complete an IPC audit?**
A: Frequency depends on your regulator and practice risk level, but ReflowAI GP schedules audits based on your setup and sends a reminder before each one is due. Your Practice Manager can adjust the schedule under **Settings → Audit Frequency**.

**Q: Can I add photos as evidence during an audit?**
A: Yes. Any question in an audit can have a photo attached. Tap the camera icon next to the question, take or upload a photo, and it is embedded in the audit record automatically.

**Q: What happens after I submit an audit?**
A: The result is saved, your score is calculated against the regulatory standard, and any failed or advisory items are surfaced as recommended actions. The AI may also suggest related tasks based on the findings.

**Q: Can I run an audit offline — for example, in a basement clinical room with no signal?**
A: Yes. Start the audit while online and complete it offline if your connection drops. The app saves your progress locally and syncs it to the server when you reconnect.

**Q: How do I view previous IPC audit results?**
A: Go to **Audits → IPC → History**. All past audits are listed with their date, score, and submitted-by name. You can open any audit to review the full detail or download it as a PDF.

## Reception

**Q: How do I log a patient complaint?**
A: Go to **Complaints → Log New**, fill in the date received, a brief description, and the patient identifier (never include full personal details in the free-text field). The system creates a tracked case and notifies the Practice Manager automatically.

**Q: What happens after I log a complaint?**
A: The platform assigns the complaint a reference number and opens a workflow that the Practice Manager will manage. Your role is complete once it is logged — you will be able to see the status of complaints you submitted under **Complaints → My Logs**.

**Q: Can I see all complaints, or only the ones I logged?**
A: Reception can see only complaints they personally logged. Practice Managers and Administrators have full access to all complaint records.

**Q: How do I complete a task assigned to me?**
A: Open the **My Tasks** screen, tap the task, fill in any required fields, and tap **Submit**. Your completion is recorded with a timestamp and becomes part of the practice's compliance record.

**Q: I submitted incorrect information on a complaint or task — what do I do?**
A: Contact your Practice Manager or Administrator immediately. You cannot edit a submitted record yourself, but a Manager can add a correction note or reopen it for amendment.

**Q: How do I log in if I have forgotten my password?**
A: On the login screen, click **Forgot Password**, enter your email address, and follow the link sent to your inbox. If you do not receive the email within a few minutes, check your junk folder or contact your Administrator.

**Q: The app is slow or showing the wrong information — what should I do?**
A: Close and reopen the app, and check your internet connection. Clearing the browser or app cache resolves most display issues. If problems continue, report it to your Practice Manager who can contact support.
